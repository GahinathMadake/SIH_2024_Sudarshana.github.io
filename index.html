Registration

Entrepreneurs and researchers will register their startup ideas and research projects by completing a comprehensive form that captures all essential information.

IPR Status Tracking using API

It enables the management and tracking of the status of startups and research projects that have applied for patents by using the API of "Official website of Intellectual Property India".

Policy Making Using Data Analytics

By analyzing the data collected from entrepreneurs and researchers post-patent grant, policymakers can use data analytics to craft informed, universal policies that address common trends and needs, ensuring more effective and broadly applicable regulations.

AI-driven Funding Recommendations

Different government and private agencies can allocate funds to startups and research projects based on AI-driven recommendations. This involves training models to analyze existing policies, business models, trends in startups, and innovations in research. By leveraging these insights, agencies can make informed funding decisions that align with current trends and potential impact.
